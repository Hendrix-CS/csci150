CSCI 150 - HW 4
Guess My Number

Overview

The purpose of this assignment is to practice the use of while loops in getting the computer to successfully play a simple game.

Description

In class, we wrote a program that allowed a human to play a game of Guess My Number: the computer randomly picks a number between 1 and 100, and the human then repeatedly guesses numbers, with the computer responding with "too small" or "too big" until the human guesses correctly. (The code written in the 8am section can be found here.)

Your assignment is to write a program that makes the computer play Guess My Number. That is, the human should pick a number between 1 and 100, and the computer will repeatedly guess numbers, with the human telling it "too small" or "too big" until the computer guesses correctly.

For example, the output of your program might look something like this:

Is your number 50? too big
Is your number 25? too small
Is your number 32? too small
Is your number 40? yes
Woohoo! I win!

Remember that you should put all your code inside function definitions, with a single call to a main() function as the last thing in the file.

Things to think about

Some extra things to think about, once you have a basic version working. You are not required to think about these to receive full credit on the assignment, but you may enjoy thinking about them:

  • Can your program detect when the human is cheating?
  • How many guesses does your program need before guessing correctly? How can you make this number as small as possible?
