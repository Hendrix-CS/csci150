Respect

  Be respectful.  Consider other ideas and points of view.

  Keep an open mind.

  Be patient


Communication

  Communicate well.  Don't talk over each other.

  Disagree constructively.  Don't discredit questions or undermine
  ideas.

Work

  Show up and do your fair share of the work.

  Work to make time to meet outside of class.  Stay together at a common pace.
